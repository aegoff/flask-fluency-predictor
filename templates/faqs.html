FluencyPredictor: FAQs



Are you storing any data? No. We don't collect any data; it's all anonymous.
Any files stored are deleted within 5 minutes.

How does this whole thing work exactly? We use offline speech-to-text recognition software that converts your audio into text.
Then, we use lexical diversity analysis to return your results to you.

How can I make the measurements be as accurate as possible?Try your best to say more than 50 words. In other words, try to use up as much of the time as you can.
