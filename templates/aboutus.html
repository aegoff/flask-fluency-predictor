FluencyPredictor: About Us




How did we do this?

We utilized the Vosk API to convert speech to text.
Then, we used lexical diversity analysis to calculate MTLD and HD-D.
Flask served as microframework for this web application, and MUI CSS served as the front-end.
No database service is being utilized for this service. Any information is deleted within 10 minutes of use.
